🍺 Font Review

Choose 1 display font for titles & headings and 1 body font for rules, labels, and UI text.
Click a card to select it. Your selection appears in the sticky bar at the bottom.

How to choose: Consider how the font feels at both large (title) and small (rule text) sizes. Body fonts must render Thai well — cards marked Thai ✓ are verified to support Thai script. Display fonts are mainly for the game title (EN version) — Thai body font will handle Thai title rendering.
